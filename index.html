My name is Scott Adler and I am a senior at Balboa High School in San Francisco. I have taken three years of coding classes through Code Nation. My classes have been centered on the langauges HTML, CSS, and Javascript (with the jQuery library). Using the skills taught to me I have created numerous websites and projects, many of which have been games.

My four favorite projects that I have created are Monaco GP, Minesweeper, a 2D collision simulator, and Conways Game of Life. Monaco GP is a clone of the 1979 Sega aracde racing game of the same name. My version of Minesweeper allows the user to choose between four different difficulties on a 10x10 grid. The collision simulator can simulate the interactions between two or three particles of various velocities and masses. The clone of Conways game of life operates on 20x20 cell board using the standard B3/S23 rules.
